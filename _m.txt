fix: show EXTRA TIME before the ET clock starts + stop stale goal overlays on resume

Bug 1: a goal arriving while the tab was hidden queued an overlay that fired
stale the moment the user returned to the foreground (a 59' goal flashed on
resume at 66'). useMatchAlerts now re-baselines (never emits) while the document
is hidden, via a new pure shouldRebaseline(hidden, gap, resumeFlag) helper --
closing the throttled-background gap the wall-clock backstop missed.

Bug 2: a level knockout at full time of regulation showed a bare "LIVE" in the
gap before extra time kicked off. mapStatus now surfaces "EXTRA TIME" when a live
match is level with no running minute and the live-endpoint Period is at/past the
second half (>= 5) -- which excludes a 0-0 at kickoff and never fires on a failed
live fetch (Period null).

Covered by new unit tests and verified headed on PC + mobile (two level knockouts
in the gap render "EXTRA TIME" instead of "LIVE").
